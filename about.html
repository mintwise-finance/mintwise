About MintWise

MintWise is a personal finance blog built for people who were never taught how money works.

Our Mission

Most schools don't teach you about credit scores, interest rates, taxes, or investing. We believe that understanding your money shouldn't require a finance degree. Every article on MintWise is written in plain English with real examples — no jargon, no fluff, no sales pitches.

What We Cover

  • Budgeting — How to track income and spending
  • Credit — Credit scores, credit cards, and debt management
  • Investing — Stocks, ETFs, index funds, and compound interest
  • Insurance — Health, auto, life, and renters insurance explained
  • Real Estate — Mortgages, renting vs buying decisions
  • Crypto — Bitcoin, Ethereum, and blockchain fundamentals
